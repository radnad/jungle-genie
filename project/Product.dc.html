Care notes

Light

Bright, indirect light keeps the white variegation crisp. A sheer-curtained east window is perfect; avoid harsh midday sun on pale leaves.

Water

Let the top 3–4cm of soil dry between drinks. She'd rather be a touch thirsty than soggy — roots dislike sitting wet.

A little extra

Loves humidity and a moss pole to climb. Wipe leaves monthly so every window of variegation can drink in the light.
