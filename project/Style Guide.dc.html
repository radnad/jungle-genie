05

The Soul — keep it on-brand

✓ Do

Warm botanical first. Gold for the wish feeling. A faint marigold sparkle near wish & bundle moments only. Encouraging, lightly enchanted copy.

✕ Don't

No cartoon genies or purple mystic clichés. No rankings, followers, or review-score UI. Never "visit us / store hours" — we're online-only.

◷ Online-only

Emphasise delivery & care. "Delivered across Egypt." Prices in EGP, lining figures, sale prices struck in marigold.
